App Features

  • Learn Telugu from English with fun, interactive games.
  • Tracing, matching, and sentence builder activities.

  • Kid-safe, ad-free, and privacy-first experience.
  • Designed by a child, for children.

Our Story & Vision

ManaTelugu was created by a 12-year-old who wanted an easy way for kids to discover Telugu while having fun. We believe language unlocks imagination. Our mission is to inspire confidence and curiosity through playful learning, in a safe, supportive space.
